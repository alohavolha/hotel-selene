Shaded by bougainvillea and blessed with a curvaceous pool and outdoor hot tubs, the romantic Suites are a honeymooner’s delight just steps from Oia’s sunny pedestrian streets.

OUR STORY

We are proud to serve our guests since 1995. The name "Selene" was chosen in honor of the Greek goddess of the moon. Selene drove the silver moon chariot which was carried by two snow-white horses.
We strive to maintain the qualities that the moon symbolically represents: power, release, quiet time, contemplation, eternity on all the levels of service.
